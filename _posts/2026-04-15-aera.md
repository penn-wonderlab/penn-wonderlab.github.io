Lab members, PhD students Jiayu Cheng and Yuwei Liang, presented two papers at the [2026 AERA conference](https://www.aera.net/AERA2026) in Los Angeles, California.
 
Papers are linked below:
 
- [**A Systematic Review of Teachers' Roles and Social-Emotional Competence in K–12 AI Research**](/file/AERA2026_cheng_slides.pdf).  Authors: Jiayu Cheng, Chen Wang and Bodong Chen
- [**Learning to Program with LLMs: How University Students Use LLMs in an Introductory Programming Course**](/file/AERA26_liang_et_al.pdf).  Authors: Yuwei Liang, Shiyang Zhang and Bodong Chen

Our collaborators also presented a poster, titled [**Supporting Justice‑Oriented Data Literacy in Science and History Classrooms**], in the *How Data Science Education Can Enable Exploration of Real World Phenomena* Symposium.
